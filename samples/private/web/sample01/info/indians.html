Free ticket information for the Cleveland Indians.

Huntington will be giving away a total of 1,200 tickets to Wednesday home games for the 2015 season. 50 pairs of free tickets will be available for each game.

Ticket giveaway details, including how and when to claim your tickets, will be announced on Huntington’s Facebook page prior to the start of the 2015 season. Check our page often so you don’t miss out!

Limit two tickets per person, per game. Must have a Huntington debit or credit card to redeem.
