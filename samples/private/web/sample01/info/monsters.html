Free ticket information for the Lake Erie Monsters.

Huntington will be giving away a total of 5,000 tickets to select hockey games. 500 pairs of free tickets will be available for each game.

Ticket giveaway details, including how and when to claim your tickets, will be announced on Huntington’s Facebook page as the games get closer. Check our page often so you don’t miss out!

Limit four tickets per person, per game. Must have a Huntington debit or credit card to redeem.
